## üìä Business Metrics & KPIs Analyzed
I implemented custom Python functions to evaluate campaign health through four primary lenses:
1.  **Efficiency Metrics:** Analyzing **CPC (Cost Per Click)** and **CPM (Cost Per Mille)** to identify cost-effective channels.
2.  **Engagement Analysis:** Evaluating **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** and **Video View Rates** to measure creative effectiveness.
3.  **Conversion Tracking:** Monitoring **Conversion Rates** and **CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)** across different platforms.
4.  **ROI Assessment:** Calculating **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)** to determine the ultimate profitability of ad spend.

## üöÄ Key Insights & Optimization Strategies
Based on the Python-driven analysis, the following strategic insights were derived:
* **Channel Performance:** Identified underperforming channels where the CPA exceeded the target threshold, leading to a recommendation for budget pivot.
* **Audience Segmentation:** Discovered high-converting audience clusters that were previously underserved, enabling more precise targeting.
* **Trend Analysis:** Mapped performance fluctuations to identify optimal delivery windows, effectively reducing wasted ad spend.
* **Creative Optimization:** Correlated high CTR with specific ad formats to guide future creative production.
